# Database Concepts

## Organization

A business using the platform.

Examples:

- Salon
- Clinic
- Restaurant
- Gym
- Spa

---

## Resource

Anything that can be reserved.

Examples:

- Barber
- Doctor
- Table
- Room
- Court

---

## Service

What the customer books.

Examples:

- Haircut
- Consultation
- Beard Trim
- Table Reservation
- Court Booking

---

## Customer

The person making the booking.

---

## Booking

Represents a reservation between a customer and one or more resources.

Examples:

- Salon Appointment
- Restaurant Reservation
- Gym Session
- Walk-in Queue
