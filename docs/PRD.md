# Product Requirements Document

## Problem Statement

Most SMEs still manage appointments using:

- Phone calls
- WhatsApp
- Paper registers
- Excel sheets

This leads to:

- Double bookings
- No-shows
- Missed appointments
- Long waiting times
- Poor customer experience

---

## Goals

Businesses should be able to:

- Create services
- Add staff/resources
- Configure business hours
- Accept appointments
- Accept walk-ins
- Check customers in
- Manage queues
- View today's schedule

---

## Non Goals

The product will NOT include:

- Inventory
- Payroll
- Medical Records
- Accounting
- CRM
- Marketing Automation

---
